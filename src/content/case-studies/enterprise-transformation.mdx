# Enterprise Digital Transformation

## The Challenge

A mid-market manufacturing company with outdated systems needed strategic guidance and technical execution to modernize their operations without disrupting their core business processes.

## Our Approach

We provided:

- Fractional CTO services to design a phased systems transformation roadmap
- Technical implementation oversight for new ERP integration
- Cross-functional team leadership bridging business and technology stakeholders

## Results

- 35% improvement in operational efficiency
- Successful migration with zero downtime
- New capabilities unlocked, leading to 18% revenue growth in new service offerings
